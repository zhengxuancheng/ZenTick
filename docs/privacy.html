Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

ZenTick is a meditation timer app designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your meditation practice is personal, and your data should stay that way.

Data Collection

ZenTick does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All your meditation session data (duration, dates, notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

Data Storage

  • On-Device Only: All session history, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your data is synced across your devices through your personal iCloud account. This data is managed by Apple and protected by your Apple ID. ZenTick developers have no access to your iCloud data.

Apple Health (Optional)

If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, ZenTick can write Mindful Minutes to Apple Health. This requires your explicit permission. ZenTick only writes data; it does not read any health data. This data stays within Apple's Health ecosystem on your device.

Notifications (Optional)

ZenTick may request permission to send local notifications for daily meditation reminders and timer completion alerts. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

ZenTick does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

ZenTick offers an optional Pro upgrade through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

ZenTick does not knowingly collect data from children. Since we collect no personal data from any user, this applies universally.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since ZenTick collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年4月14日

概述

ZenTick 是一款以隐私保护为核心原则的冥想计时器应用。我们认为您的冥想练习是私人的，您的数据也应当如此。

数据收集

ZenTick 不收集、传输或分享任何个人数据。

您的所有冥想记录（时长、日期、备注）均使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架存储在您的设备本地。我们没有服务器、没有数据分析、没有用户追踪。

数据存储

  • 仅限设备本地：所有的冥想记录、设置和偏好都存储在您的设备上。
  • iCloud 同步（可选）：如果您在设置中开启 iCloud 同步，您的数据将通过您的个人 iCloud 账户在设备间同步。此数据由 Apple 管理，受您的 Apple ID 保护。ZenTick 开发者无权访问您的 iCloud 数据。

Apple 健康（可选）

如果您选择启用 HealthKit 集成，ZenTick 可以将正念分钟数写入 Apple 健康。这需要您的明确授权。ZenTick 仅写入数据，不读取任何健康数据。此数据保留在您设备上的 Apple 健康生态系统中。

通知（可选）

ZenTick 可能请求发送本地通知的权限，用于每日冥想提醒和计时器结束提示。这些通知完全在您的设备上生成和调度，不会向任何外部服务器发送数据。

第三方服务

ZenTick 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。唯一使用的外部服务是 Apple 的 StoreKit（用于应用内购买），该服务受 Apple 自身的隐私政策约束。

应用内购买

ZenTick 通过 Apple App Store 提供可选的 Pro 升级。所有支付处理由 Apple 负责。我们无法访问您的支付信息。

儿童隐私

ZenTick 不会有意收集儿童的数据。由于我们不收集任何用户的个人数据，这一条适用于所有用户。

政策变更

如果我们更新此隐私政策，变更将发布在此页面并附上更新日期。由于 ZenTick 不收集数据，我们预计不会有重大变更。
